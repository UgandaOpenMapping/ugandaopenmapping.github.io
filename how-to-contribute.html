Find out about how you can get involved in the Uganda Open Mapping project!

  • If you are new to collaborative mapping and you want to have more details about OpenStreetMap and its community, you can start from here: About OpenStreetMap
  • If are an individual citizen without mapping background and you would like to contribute to OSM, have a look at the OSM Beginners' guide and this video tutorial on "How to sign up for OSM".

For more details on the specific mapping needs of the Uganda Open Mapping Program, please check the related Tasks in the HOT Tasking Manager.
