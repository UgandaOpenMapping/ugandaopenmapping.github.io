Open and collaborative data collection
leveraging the OpenStreetMap (OSM) open data platform.

Logos Here.

Capacity Development

Organize workshops and on-site training for central and local government office staff on OSM-related tools, mapping workflows, data collection mobile apps and data models.

Mapping

Remote and field mapping of key priority areas identified by the Government of Uganda, to collect disaster risk management related data and information.

UAV

Introduce the use of innovative technology such as Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) and street-level photo mapping to local government staff and community members, with the goal to collect high resolution imagery to be used for detailed OSM mapping.

Expected outcomes

Significantly increased coverage and accuracy of OSM data in target areas in Uganda
Developed technical capacity of Government of Uganda staff and community member in using open mapping tools
Demonstrated use of innovative imaging technologies for supporting community mapping activities

  • Team

    Geo Gecko, the Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Team (HOT), ITHACA and MapUganda have teamed up together for this project.

  • Local Stakeholders

    The Uganda Bureau of Statistics (UBOS) and the Kampala Capital City Authority

  • Capacity Development

    Workshops and on-site training for central and local government office staff on OSM-related tools, mapping workflows, data collection mobile apps and data models.

  • Mapping

    Remote and field mapping of key priority areas identified by the Government of Uganda, to collect disaster risk management related data and information.

  • UAV Imagery

    Introduce the use of innovative technology such as Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) and street-level photo mapping to local government staff and community members.

  • From UAV to OAM

    Collect high resolution imagery to be used for detailed OSM mapping and shared in OpenAerialMap (OAM).

Mapping status

Interactive maps to illustrate the overall mapped area, with statistics of mapping activity (e.g. km of mapped roads, number of buildings, etc).
